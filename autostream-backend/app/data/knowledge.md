## Common Questions

### What platforms do you support?
We support content from YouTube, Twitch, Facebook Gaming, TikTok, Instagram, and direct uploads.

### Can I upgrade or downgrade?
Yes! You can change plans at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

### Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all plans. No questions asked.

### What's included in AI editing?
Our AI automatically:
- Removes dead air and awkward pauses
- Adds smooth transitions
- Detects and highlights key moments
- Balances audio levels
- (Pro only) Generates accurate captions in 20+ languages

### How long does processing take?
- Basic Plan: 10-15 minutes for a 10-minute video
- Pro Plan: 5-8 minutes for a 10-minute video (priority processing)

### Is there a free trial?
Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial of the Pro Plan. No credit card required.

## Policies

### Cancellation Policy
You can cancel anytime. Your plan remains active until the end of your billing period.

### Usage Limits
- Basic: 10 exports/month (resets on billing date)
- Pro: Unlimited (fair use policy: no reselling or commercial redistribution)

### Data Retention
We keep your uploaded content for 30 days after export. Pro users get 90 days.

### Privacy
We never share your content with third parties. All processing is done on secure servers. See our full privacy policy at autostream.ai/privacy
